Demo: Wifi ServiceWorker

1. This is the regular content. All is well. You are online or the cache of the website is still in your browser. This is how most websites work.


2. Disconnect your internet connection. Give this website a 'normal' or 'soft' refresh. It will likely remain the same due to being cached.


3. Give the website a hard refresh i.e:

  • * Windows: ctrl + F5
  • * Mac/Apple: Apple + R or command + R
  • * Linux: F5

4. You may see a message saying it cannot load the page because of no internet connection upon doing this the first time as you clear the cache. You may even see the 'Fail T-Rex' just the one time as the Cache doesn't come and help you out. Do not be afraid! visit this URL again and you should see offline page with a comforting orange background. Magic!
